privacy policy ×

what we collect
nothing. stak.dev has no accounts, no login, no cookies, no analytics, no tracking scripts.

search queries
when you search for a package, the query is forwarded to the npm registry via our proxy. we do not log or store these queries.

shareable urls
your package selections are stored only in the url itself. we have no database. when you share a link, the recipient can see what packages you selected — that's it.

cloudflare
the site is hosted on cloudflare. cloudflare may collect basic network-level data (ip, request metadata) per their own privacy policy.
